📜 How to Play

1
Setup

Enter 4 player names and choose the number of rounds.

2
Draw Chits

4 roles are shuffled: Raja (King), Rani (Queen), Police, and Thirudan (Thief). Each player secretly views their role.

3
Police Guesses

The Police reveals themselves and must identify the Thief from the remaining players.

4
Scoring

👑 Raja: 1000 pts always
💎 Rani: 500 pts always
🛡️ Police: 800 pts if correct, 0 pts if wrong
🦹 Thief: 0 pts if caught, 800 pts if escapes

5
Winner

After all rounds, the player with the highest score wins! 🏆
